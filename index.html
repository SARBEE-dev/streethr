About

STREET HR HUB is an HR broadcasting platform where all HR and related matters are discussed. The platform aims at dissolving any/every doubt around the HR profession through educative, expository and interactive sessions using both online and offline mediums.

    OUR VISION

  • To be a Global Broadcasting Platform that provides insights on HR matters.

    OUR MISSION

  • To continually create an educational avenue where HR professionals and enthusiasts can have discourse
    on issues surrounding the HR field and to provide a forum that connects all the parties in the HR space/community.
