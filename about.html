STREET HR HUB is an HR broadcasting platform where all HR and related matters are discussed. The platform aims at dissolving any/every doubt around the HR profession through educative, expository and interactive sessions using both online and offline mediums.

    OUR VISION

  • To be a Global Broadcasting Platform that provides insights on HR matters.

    OUR MISSION

  • To continually create an educational avenue where HR professionals and enthusiasts can have discourse
    on issues surrounding the HR field and to provide a forum that connects all the parties in the HR space/community.

    OUR CORE VALUES

  • Professionalism
  • Integrity and Respect
  • Commitment to Providing more Recognition for HR Profession
  • Commitment to Excellence in Creating an Educational Platform
  • Promotion of Ethical Conduct

    OUR GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

  • To create a platform for discussion of pertinent HR matters.
  • To create a platform where people who are looking to carve a career in HR, those willing to switch career as well as current HR professional can access recent trends in the field.
  • To establish a platform where people nationwide and internationally can know more about HR and appreciate the field.
  • To bring HR professionals across different industries to discuss matters surrounding the challenges, solutions and prospects of HR in their respective industries.
  • To create a deliberate learning environment for HR students, personnel, enthusiast and others.
  • We are also creating an HR Hub (a web-based application) to foster interconnectedness, mentoring and learning platform that connect professionals, practitioners, personnel, students, enthusiasts and others in HR field and the entire HR community.
